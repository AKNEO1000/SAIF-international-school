Our Curriculum:

  • Holistic Education: A balanced curriculum that emphasizes both academic rigor and personal development.
  • Experienced Educators: A team of highly qualified and dedicated teachers committed to student success.
  • Practical learning: students learn highly valuable skills that are used in the real world.
  • No restriction at speaking in the class. As long as the students are discussing results that are topic-related, students can share there knowledge with each other.
  • Examinations: The only examinations are the final exams. Students will be exposed to exams, starting from 5th grade.
